
== Installation ==
1. Upload the entire `wp_trigger` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
You will find 'WP_Triggers' menu in your WordPress admin panel.
For basic usage, you can also have a look at the [plugin homepage](http://www.webdesignersacademy.com/).
